A Journey Through Nature

Cameron Clair

Nature is where I belong. I spend a considerable amount of my free time exploring nature. Walking through the woods surrounded by silence quiets a busy mind. I climbed around 15 mountains last semester clocking around 120 miles. I look forward to this summer as I plan to hike the Continental Divide. With the short time frame I will need to average around 29 miles a day to complete the 3100 mile trail on time, not sure I can do it but I love a good challenge.
